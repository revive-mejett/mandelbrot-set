Info

This is called the Mandelbrot set. It is based on the iterative equation Z = (Zn-1)2 + C The coordinates are plotted on plane where the x coordinate represents the real numbers and the y (i) axis represents the numbers on the complex plane. The idea is that a coordinate is iterated multiple times and it is coloured based on whether the complex numbers go towards infinity (not in the set, coloured based on the number of iterations taken) or not (in the mandelbrot set, coloured black).
